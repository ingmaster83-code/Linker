About

Wavve is South Korea's leading OTT service, formed by a partnership between SKT and the three major terrestrial broadcasters — KBS, MBC, and SBS. It offers live TV streaming, catch-up VOD for all major broadcast dramas and variety shows, and Wavve Originals. It also carries a selection of international dramas and films. Wavve is essential for anyone following Korean broadcast TV.

💡 Tips

  • If you're on SKT's mobile network, check whether your plan includes a free Wavve subscription — many SKT plans bundle Wavve access.
  • New drama and variety show episodes are usually available for catch-up within 1–2 hours of the original broadcast time.
  • For tvN or JTBC content (e.g. Reply 1988, Crash Landing on You), use TVING instead — Wavve focuses on KBS, MBC, and SBS.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free tier?

Some older content is available free with ads. However, recent broadcasts and Wavve Originals require a paid subscription.

How does Wavve differ from TVING?

Wavve focuses on the three major broadcasters (KBS, MBC, SBS), while TVING specialises in CJ ENM channels like tvN, OCN, and Mnet. Your choice depends on which shows you watch.

Can I access Wavve outside South Korea?

Wavve is restricted to South Korean IP addresses. Access from abroad is generally not supported under the terms of service.
