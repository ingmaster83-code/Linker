About

Trello is a kanban board tool organized into Boards, Lists, and Cards. Drag cards between lists to represent progress, add checklists and due dates, and collaborate with teammates in real time. Simple enough for personal to-do lists, flexible enough for complex team projects.

Key Features

  • 📋 Kanban Board
  • 🃏 Drag & Drop Cards
  • 👥 Team Collaboration
  • 📅 Due Dates
  • 🏷️ Labels & Filters
  • 🔌 Power-Ups
  • 📱 Mobile App
  • 🤖 Butler Automation

💡 Tips

  • Add a checklist inside a card to break large tasks into subtasks and track granular progress without creating separate cards.
  • Use Butler automations to auto-assign members, send notifications, or move cards when specific triggers occur.
  • Color-code labels and card covers to visually distinguish priorities or project types at a glance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trello free?

The free plan includes up to 10 boards and core features. Unlimited boards and advanced features require Standard at $5/user/month.

How does it differ from Jira?

Trello is visual and beginner-friendly, great for non-developers. Jira is specialized for software development teams managing sprints and complex issue workflows.

Does it integrate with other tools?

Yes — Slack, Google Drive, GitHub, Salesforce, and hundreds of others via Power-Ups.
