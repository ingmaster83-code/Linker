About

Flaticon is the world's largest vector icon library with over 16 million icons. Download in SVG, PNG, PSD, or EPS formats, or generate an icon font for web projects. An online editor lets you change colors and sizes before downloading. Icons are organized by style and category for easy browsing.

💡 Tips

  • Click the Edit button on any icon to change its color directly in the browser before downloading — no design tool needed.
  • Free downloads require attribution to Flaticon.com. A premium subscription removes the attribution requirement for commercial use.
  • Download icon packs to keep a consistent visual style across all icons in your project.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flaticon free?

Yes, with required attribution (credit to Flaticon.com). A premium plan ($9.99/month+) removes attribution requirements for commercial projects.

Can I use icons commercially?

Free-tier icons can be used commercially with attribution. Premium subscribers can use icons without attribution.

Are SVG files available?

Yes — SVG, PNG, PSD, and EPS are all available. SVG is the most popular format as it scales perfectly at any size.
