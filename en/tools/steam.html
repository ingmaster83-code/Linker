About

Steam is Valve's digital game distribution platform and the dominant PC gaming storefront. It offers 50,000+ games spanning all genres, with seasonal sales (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter) offering up to 90% off. Thousands of free-to-play titles are available at no cost. Steam Workshop lets players create and share mods, and the Steam Deck handheld gaming PC runs the full Steam library.

💡 Tips

  • Add games to your Wishlist and Steam will email you when they go on sale — avoid paying full price by waiting for the seasonal sale.
  • Check SteamDB (steamdb.info) to see the historical low price for any game before buying — useful for judging whether a current discount is genuinely good value.
  • Set up Steam Family Sharing to let household members access your game library on their own accounts (one person playing at a time).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a Steam account free?

Yes, creating a Steam account is free. You can play all free-to-play games at no cost. Paid games require a one-time purchase.

Can I refund a game I don't like?

Steam's refund policy allows returns within 14 days of purchase and under 2 hours of playtime, no questions asked. Request it through the Steam support page.

How do I check if a game will run on my PC?

Every game's store page lists minimum and recommended specs. Steam also automatically compares those specs against your PC hardware and shows a compatibility indicator.
