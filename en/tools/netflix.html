About

Netflix is the world's leading OTT streaming service with 270+ million subscribers. Its award-winning original content — including Squid Game, Stranger Things, and The Crown — is available in 190+ countries. Netflix offers multiple pricing tiers from a low-cost ad-supported plan to a 4K Premium plan. The service includes offline downloads, multiple profiles, and Netflix Games at no extra charge on most plans.

💡 Tips

  • Use thumbs-up and thumbs-down ratings aggressively — the more you rate, the better the recommendation algorithm learns your taste and stops suggesting irrelevant content.
  • Downloaded content stays available offline for up to 30 days, but once you start watching, you have 7 days to finish before it expires.
  • Search Netflix genre codes (e.g. "N7120") to access hidden sub-genre categories not visible in regular browsing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Netflix no longer offers free trials in most countries. The cheapest entry point is the ad-supported Standard with Ads plan at $6.99/month.

Can I share my account with people outside my household?

Since 2023, Netflix has restricted sharing outside a household. You can add one extra member for an additional fee ($7.99/mo in the US) on Standard or Premium plans.

Which plan supports 4K streaming?

Only the Premium plan ($22.99/mo) supports 4K UHD + HDR streaming. Standard and lower plans cap out at 1080p Full HD.
