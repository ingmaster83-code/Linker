About

Spotify is a Swedish music streaming service used by over 600 million people globally. It offers 100 million+ songs and 5 million+ podcasts. The free tier lets you listen with ads and shuffle play; Premium ($9.99/mo) removes ads, enables offline downloads, high-quality audio (320 kbps), and on-demand track selection. Unique features like AI DJ, Discover Weekly, and the annual Spotify Wrapped have made it synonymous with music discovery.

💡 Tips

  • Check your "Discover Weekly" playlist every Monday — Spotify generates a fresh mix of 30 songs tailored to your taste. It's one of the best free features for music discovery.
  • The Family plan ($15.99/mo) covers up to 6 individual accounts, making it significantly cheaper than 6 separate Premium subscriptions.
  • Search for a BPM number (e.g. "BPM 150") to find playlists matched to your running pace or workout intensity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free: ads between songs, shuffle-only playback, no offline mode. Premium: no ads, on-demand song selection, offline downloads, and high-quality 320 kbps audio.

How does Spotify compare to Apple Music or YouTube Music?

Spotify leads in algorithmic recommendations (Discover Weekly, AI DJ) and social features. Apple Music integrates tightly with Apple devices. YouTube Music includes music videos. Choose based on your ecosystem and preference.

Can I listen to podcasts on the free plan?

Yes — all podcasts are free with no ads. Free users can even download podcast episodes for offline listening, which is one of Spotify's most generous free-tier perks.
