About

YouTube is Google's video platform and the world's second-largest search engine. Over 500 hours of video are uploaded every minute, covering education, music, gaming, news, cooking, travel, and virtually every other topic imaginable. It's free with ads, and YouTube Premium ($13.99/mo) removes ads, enables background play, and includes YouTube Music. Creators can also monetize their content, making it the go-to platform for independent media.

💡 Tips

  • Speed up long lectures or podcasts by pressing < or > to decrease/increase playback speed. 1.5× or 2× is often comfortable once you get used to it.
  • Use search filters (Upload date + Subtitles/CC) to find recent videos with captions — great for language learning.
  • After subscribing to a channel, click the bell icon 🔔 and select "All" to ensure you never miss a new upload notification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is YouTube Premium worth it?

If you watch a lot of YouTube and find ads disruptive, or if you listen to YouTube with your phone screen off, Premium is worth it. It also bundles YouTube Music, effectively replacing a separate music streaming subscription. A Family plan ($22.99/mo) covers up to 5 members.

Can I download YouTube videos for offline viewing?

YouTube Premium subscribers can save videos offline in the mobile app. Free users cannot download videos natively. Some creators share download links directly on their websites or via platforms like Patreon.

What are YouTube Shorts?

YouTube Shorts are vertical videos up to 60 seconds long, similar to TikTok and Instagram Reels. They appear in a dedicated Shorts feed and are favored by the algorithm for discovery, making them popular for creators looking to grow their audience quickly.
