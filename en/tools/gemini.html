About

Gemini is Google's conversational AI built on the Gemini 1.5 model family. It connects to Google Search for real-time answers and integrates seamlessly with Google Workspace. It also handles multimodal inputs, understanding both text and images.

Key Features

  • 📝 Writing & Summarization
  • 💻 Coding Support
  • 🌐 Real-time Web Search
  • 📊 Image Analysis
  • 📧 Gmail & Docs Integration
  • 🎨 Image Generation
  • 🔍 Google Search
  • 🌍 Multilingual

💡 Tips

  • A Google account is all you need to start for free. Connect to Gmail for AI-assisted email drafting.
  • For live news or up-to-date facts, Gemini's Google Search integration gives it an edge over static-training models.
  • Gemini Advanced unlocks Gemini 1.5 Pro for longer document analysis and more complex reasoning tasks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini free?

The base Gemini is free with a Google account. Gemini Advanced with 1.5 Pro is available through Google One AI Premium at $19.99/month.

How is it different from ChatGPT?

Gemini excels at Google service integration and live search. ChatGPT has a broader plugin ecosystem and strong code-execution tools.

Is my data safe?

Data is governed by Google's privacy policy. You can turn off activity saving in Gemini settings to opt out of model training.
