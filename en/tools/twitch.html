About

Twitch, owned by Amazon, is the world's leading live streaming platform. It started as a gaming platform but has expanded to music, talk shows, cooking, sports, and more. Watching is free; you can chat in real time with streamers and their community. Channel subscriptions (from $4.99/mo) let you support your favorite streamers and unlock perks like emotes and ad-free viewing for that channel.

💡 Tips

  • Browse by category (game or content type) to discover popular channels in your area of interest. The Categories tab lists all active streams sorted by viewer count.
  • Earn Channel Points just by watching — no cost. Accumulate them to redeem streamer-set rewards like chat highlights or custom emotes.
  • Amazon Prime subscribers get one free monthly channel subscription via Prime Gaming. Check the Prime Gaming tab in the Twitch navigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Twitch completely free to watch?

Yes — watching any stream and participating in chat is 100% free. Channel subscriptions are optional and exist to directly support your favourite streamers.

How long are VODs kept?

Standard streamers keep VODs for 14 days. Twitch Affiliates and Partners can retain VODs for up to 60 days. Streamers can also export highlights to YouTube for permanent archiving.

Can I filter streams by language?

Yes. Use the language filter on the directory page to show only streams in your preferred language.
