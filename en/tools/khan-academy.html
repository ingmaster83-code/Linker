About

Khan Academy is a non-profit educational organisation with the mission of providing "a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere." Founded by Sal Khan, it offers comprehensive free courses in maths (K–12 through university level), science, history, economics, and computer programming. Everything is completely free with no ads. It also provides the official SAT preparation program in partnership with College Board.

💡 Tips

  • If you have gaps in foundational maths, start from the elementary level and work up — Khan Academy's mastery-based progression identifies exactly where your weaknesses are.
  • Use the official SAT prep program (a College Board partnership) if you're preparing for the SAT — it's free, uses real practice questions, and personalises your study plan.
  • Parents can create a parent account linked to their child's to monitor learning progress, time spent, and areas needing improvement from a dedicated dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Khan Academy really completely free?

Yes — 100% free forever. No ads, no paywalls, no premium tiers. Khan Academy is funded entirely by donations and grants.

What age/level is it appropriate for?

Khan Academy serves learners from kindergarten through early college. Its maths content is particularly comprehensive, covering everything from counting to calculus and linear algebra.

Does Khan Academy offer certificates?

Khan Academy does not offer accredited certificates or qualifications. It is best used for self-study and supplementary learning. The SAT prep program is an official College Board partnership but is itself not a standalone credential.
