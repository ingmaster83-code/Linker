About

Daum News is the news service of Daum, Kakao's web portal and one of the two dominant Korean news aggregators alongside Naver News. It collects articles from hundreds of Korean media outlets and organises them by topic. Integration with Kakao accounts and KakaoTalk makes it easy to share articles and subscribe to specific journalists or outlets for a personalised reading experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Daum News different from Naver News?

Both aggregate Korean media, but Daum News integrates more deeply with the Kakao ecosystem (KakaoTalk sharing, Kakao account login). They use different recommendation algorithms, so some users prefer one over the other for discovering news.

Is Daum News available in English?

The content is in Korean. For English-language Korean news, visit Korea Herald or Korea JoongAng Daily instead.

Can I access Daum News on mobile?

Yes. Access it through a mobile browser or the Kakao app's built-in news tab, which is linked to your KakaoTalk account.
