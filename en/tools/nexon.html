About

Nexon, founded in 1994, is South Korea's largest gaming company by revenue. It publishes dozens of popular online games including MapleStory, Dungeon Fighter Online (DNF), KartRider, and Mabinogi. Games are available in South Korea, North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. Most titles follow a free-to-play model with optional cosmetic purchases. The official Nexon website is the authorised source for game clients and updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Nexon games free?

Most Nexon titles are free to download and play. Optional cash items (cosmetics, convenience features) can be purchased but gameplay is generally not pay-to-win.

Can I play Nexon games from outside South Korea?

Several titles have global servers (e.g., MapleStory Global, DNF). However, some Korea-exclusive services may restrict overseas access.

What payment methods does Nexon accept?

Nexon accepts credit cards, bank transfers, and prepaid NX cards available at convenience stores and online retailers.
