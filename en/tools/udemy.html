About

Udemy is one of the world's largest online learning platforms with 67+ million learners. It offers 200,000+ courses across development, data science, business, marketing, and design. While list prices can appear high, Udemy runs deep discounts constantly — most courses are available for $10–20 during sales. All purchases include lifetime access, offline downloads, and a completion certificate.

💡 Tips

  • Udemy is almost always running a sitewide sale. Never pay list price — wait a day or check coupon sites and you'll find most courses for $10–15.
  • Before buying, check the rating (aim for 4.5+), enrolment count (10,000+ is a good signal), and the last-updated date — older courses may not cover current tools.
  • Udemy's 30-day refund policy means you can request a full refund if a course doesn't meet expectations — just don't abuse it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there always sales on Udemy?

Yes — Udemy almost permanently runs sitewide discount events. New visitors, newsletter subscribers, and even just waiting a few days will typically surface courses at $10–20 regardless of the listed price.

Are courses available in languages other than English?

Primarily English, but a growing number of courses are available in Korean, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish, and other languages. Use the language filter to find courses in your preferred language.

Is the Udemy certificate worth anything?

Udemy certificates are not accredited qualifications. They're useful for demonstrating self-driven learning on a résumé or portfolio, but shouldn't be confused with industry certifications like AWS, Google, or CompTIA.
