About

Unsplash is a free photo platform with millions of high-resolution images contributed by photographers worldwide. All photos are free for personal and commercial use under the Unsplash License, with no mandatory attribution — though crediting the photographer is appreciated. It's a go-to resource for websites, blogs, apps, and presentations.

Key Features

  • 📸 Millions of HD Photos
  • 🆓 Free Commercial Use
  • 🔍 Keyword Search
  • 📁 Collection Saving
  • 🎨 Color & Mood Filters
  • 📱 Unsplash API
  • ⬇️ Full-res Downloads
  • 🤝 Photographer Credit

💡 Tips

  • Search in English for best results — try descriptive terms like 'minimal workspace' or 'blue abstract texture' for more targeted results.
  • Crediting the photographer isn't required but is encouraged as a community norm.
  • Use the Unsplash API to pull photos directly into your app or website without manual downloading.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, for personal and commercial use. The one restriction is you cannot resell Unsplash photos as part of a competing stock photo service.

Do I need to credit the photographer?

Attribution is not required by the license, but linking back to the photographer and Unsplash is encouraged.

Can I download full resolution?

Yes, every photo is available for free download at its original full resolution.
