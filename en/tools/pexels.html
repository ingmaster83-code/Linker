About

Pexels provides millions of free high-quality photos and video clips contributed by photographers and videographers worldwide. All content is free for personal and commercial use under the Pexels License, with no mandatory attribution. Compared to Unsplash, Pexels also offers a large library of free video clips.

Key Features

  • 📸 Millions of HD Photos
  • 🎬 Free Video Clips
  • 🆓 No Credit Required
  • 🔍 Keyword Search
  • 🎨 Color Filter Search
  • 📱 Mobile App
  • ⬇️ Multiple Resolutions
  • 📋 Curated Collections

💡 Tips

  • Don't overlook the video section — free clips are available for social content, presentations, and video projects.
  • Use the color filter to find photos matching your brand palette by dominant color.
  • The Pexels API lets you embed photos and videos directly in your app or website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, all photos and videos are completely free for personal and commercial use.

Is attribution required?

No, attribution is not required. Crediting the photographer is optional but appreciated.

How is it different from Unsplash?

Pexels also offers a large library of free video clips, while Unsplash is photo-only. Using both sites gives you the widest selection.
