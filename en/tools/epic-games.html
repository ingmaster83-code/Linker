About

Epic Games Store is the PC gaming storefront from Epic Games, the creator of Fortnite and Unreal Engine. Its standout feature is the weekly free game program — every Thursday, 1–2 paid games are offered completely free to claim. Past giveaways have included GTA V, Cyberpunk 2077, Control, and many other high-value titles. Epic also offers developers an 88% revenue share, more generous than most competitors.

💡 Tips

  • New free games drop every Thursday. Turn on Epic Games app notifications or follow their social accounts so you never miss a giveaway.
  • You don't need to install the free game immediately — just add it to your library during the free window to own it permanently, and download whenever you want.
  • Epic's 88% revenue share for developers means indie studios keep more money, so buying directly on Epic is often a more developer-friendly choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the free games really free?

Yes, 100% free. Add the game to your library during the free period and it's yours permanently — no subscription required.

Can I use Epic Games alongside Steam?

Absolutely. Epic Games Launcher and Steam are separate platforms that coexist on the same PC. Many games are available on both stores.

What notable games have been given away free?

Past giveaways include GTA V, Cyberpunk 2077, Control, Among Us, The Witcher 3 DLC, and hundreds of other titles worth billions of dollars in total.
