About

The Verge, published by Vox Media, is one of the most-read technology news outlets globally. It covers AI, consumer electronics, gaming, automotive tech, and science, with a focus on both breaking news and long-form analysis exploring technology's impact on society. Its product reviews are particularly well-regarded for depth and independence.

💡 Tips

  • The Verge's Reviews section is essential reading before buying any consumer electronics — their in-depth tests go beyond spec sheets to real-world usage.
  • Subscribe to The Verge's weekly newsletter for a curated summary of the most important tech stories of the week, delivered to your inbox every Friday.
  • The Decoder podcast features long-form CEO interviews that reveal the business logic behind major tech industry decisions — great for understanding industry dynamics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Verge free to read?

Most content is free. The Verge occasionally publishes premium or paywalled content, but the majority of news and reviews are accessible without a subscription.

How often is The Verge updated?

The Verge publishes dozens of articles daily, covering breaking news in real time alongside weekly long-form features and product reviews.

How trustworthy are The Verge's reviews?

The Verge maintains editorial independence from advertisers and is generally considered one of the most credible consumer tech review outlets in the industry.
