About

Inflearn is South Korea's largest e-learning platform specialising in IT and software development. It hosts tens of thousands of courses on web development, Python, data science, machine learning, DevOps, and more. A significant portion of courses are free, making it accessible to beginners. Korean developers and instructors create practical, job-focused content used by students and working professionals alike. Learning roadmaps help you plan your study path.

💡 Tips

  • Always watch the free preview lessons before buying — it's the best way to verify the instructor's teaching style matches how you learn.
  • Inflearn runs irregular sale campaigns and bundle discounts. Add courses to your wishlist to be notified when prices drop.
  • Free courses alone can teach you the fundamentals of web development, Python, and Linux — build your foundation before investing in paid content.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do courses expire?

Most paid courses offer lifetime access with no expiry. Some subscription-based content may differ — check before purchasing.

How do I get a completion certificate?

Complete at least 80% of a course to receive a certificate. Find it under My Page → My Learning → Certificates.

How does Inflearn compare to Udemy?

Inflearn excels in Korean-language practical content aimed at the Korean job market. Udemy offers a wider global catalogue in English with deeper discounts. Use both for the broadest coverage.
