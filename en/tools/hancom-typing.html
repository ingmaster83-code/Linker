About

Hancom Typing (한컴타자연습) is a free typing practice service officially operated by Hancom, the maker of Korea's widely used Hangul word processor. It used to require installing a desktop program, but now offers a web version at hancomtaja.com that runs directly in the browser — no installation needed. It's designed for learners of all ages, from elementary school students to adults, with a 5-stage curriculum: key-position drills, word practice, short-text practice, long-text practice, and typing games.

💡 Tips

  • If you're new to Korean typing, don't skip straight to word or long-text practice — start with key-position drills first to build proper finger placement, which pays off much faster in the long run.
  • If you need the installable desktop version, only download it from the official domains (hancomtaja.com, hancom.com). Third-party sites ranking high in search results for this program are not official and may bundle malware.
  • Short, consistent daily practice sessions improve typing speed more reliably than occasional long sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use it without installing anything?

Yes. The current web version runs directly in your browser, so you can start practicing immediately just by visiting the site — no download required.

Where can I get the installable PC version?

If you need an offline desktop version, it's available through the official site's download menu. Only download it from the official domains (hancomtaja.com, hancom.com) to avoid unofficial copies.

Is it really completely free?

Yes — it's an official free service from Hancom, and most practice features are available without needing to create an account.
