About

TechCrunch, founded in 2005, is the definitive media outlet for startup and venture capital news. It covers funding rounds, acquisitions, product launches, and strategic moves by both emerging startups and Big Tech. Founders, investors, and anyone interested in the business of technology should check it daily. TechCrunch+ (paid) provides deeper analysis, exclusive interviews, and proprietary venture data.

💡 Tips

  • Use the "Funding" filter to browse investment news sorted by round stage (Seed, Series A, B, etc.) — useful for tracking which sectors are attracting capital.
  • Pair TechCrunch with Crunchbase to dive deeper into company and investor profiles behind the funding stories.
  • TechCrunch Disrupt is an annual flagship event featuring startup pitch competitions. Recordings are available online after the event.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in TC+?

TC+ (~$15/mo) unlocks in-depth analysis articles, exclusive founder and investor interviews, and proprietary venture market data not available to free readers.

How often is TechCrunch updated?

TechCrunch publishes breaking news throughout the day. Funding announcements and major product launches are typically covered within hours.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, iOS and Android apps are available. You can set up topic-specific push notifications to get alerted when news in your area of interest is published.
