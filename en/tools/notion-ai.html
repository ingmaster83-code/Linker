About

Notion AI is the AI layer built into the Notion productivity platform. Invoke it inside any page to draft writing, summarize documents, translate text, generate to-do lists, and analyze databases — all without switching to a separate AI tool. The key advantage is that it works within the context of your existing Notion workspace.

Key Features

  • ✍️ Writing Drafts
  • 📄 Document Summarization
  • 🌐 Translation
  • ✅ To-do Generation
  • 📊 Data Analysis
  • 💬 Q&A
  • 🗃️ Database Automation
  • 🔍 Workspace Search

💡 Tips

  • Select any text on a page and click 'Ask AI' to instantly summarize, translate, or improve the selected content.
  • On a blank page, press Space to invoke AI and ask it to write the first draft for you.
  • In databases, use AI to auto-categorize entries or generate a summary column for each row.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notion AI free?

Notion AI costs an additional $8/user/month (or $10 billed monthly) on top of any Notion plan, including the free plan.

How is it different from ChatGPT?

Notion AI can reference your existing workspace documents and databases as context, giving you responses tailored to your own content — something standalone AI tools can't do.

What languages does it support?

It supports many languages including Korean and English, with built-in translation features useful for multilingual document work.
