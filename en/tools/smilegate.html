About

Smilegate is a South Korean game developer founded in 2002, known for CrossFire, Lost Ark, and Epic Seven. Lost Ark achieved the second-highest concurrent player peak in Steam history at launch in 2022, driven by its polished MMORPG gameplay. CrossFire is dominant in China and Southeast Asia with hundreds of millions of registered players. Epic Seven is a popular gacha RPG with anime-style art.

💡 Tips

  • Lost Ark is available on Steam — you can start with your existing Steam account without creating a separate Smilegate account.
  • Epic Seven runs regular limited-time collaborations (past collabs include TFT and Jujutsu Kaisen). Follow the official social channels so you don't miss time-limited content.
  • CrossFire has a much larger active playerbase in Southeast Asia and China than in Western regions — factor this into server selection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lost Ark different between Korean and Global servers?

Yes. The Korean version (operated by Smilegate directly) and the Global version (operated by Amazon Games) have different update schedules and some content differences.

Where can I play Epic Seven?

Epic Seven is available on iOS and Android. PC players can use an Android emulator. Download links are on the official Smilegate website.

How do I follow Smilegate's new game announcements?

Check the official Smilegate website's Games section and follow their official YouTube and social channels for development updates and new title announcements.
