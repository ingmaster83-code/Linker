About

Midjourney is an AI image generation service that creates high-quality images from text prompts. Renowned for its artistic output quality, it's popular among designers, artists, and marketers. You can access it through its web interface or via Discord.

Key Features

  • 🖼️ AI Image Generation
  • ✏️ Style Customization
  • 🔄 Variations & Upscaling
  • 📐 Aspect Ratio Control
  • 🎭 Multiple Art Styles
  • 👁️ Image-to-Image
  • 💾 High-res Downloads
  • 🌐 Web Interface

💡 Tips

  • Append parameters to your prompt: '--ar 16:9' for aspect ratio, '--v 6' for model version, '--style raw' for more literal interpretation.
  • Be specific about style: 'oil painting, impressionist, soft lighting, warm tones' yields far better results than vague descriptions.
  • Use the V (Variation) buttons on any result to explore similar options, or U (Upscale) to get a high-resolution version.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Midjourney free?

There is currently no free plan. Paid plans start at $10/month (Basic), with Standard and Pro tiers offering more GPU time and features.

Do English prompts work better?

Yes, significantly. While other languages work, English prompts give you far more control and variety. Detailed English descriptions produce the best results.

Who owns the generated images?

Paid subscribers can use generated images commercially. Always review the Midjourney Terms of Service for the latest usage rights.
