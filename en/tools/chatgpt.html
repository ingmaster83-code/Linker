About

ChatGPT is a conversational AI service developed by OpenAI. Ask it anything in natural language and it helps with drafting text, generating and debugging code, translating documents, summarizing content, and brainstorming ideas. Launched in November 2022, it became the fastest-growing AI service in history and is now used by hundreds of millions of people worldwide.

  • 💻 Coding & Debugging

💡 Tips

  • The more specific your prompt, the better the answer. Instead of "write Python code to read CSV", try "write Python code using pandas to load a CSV, filter rows where column X > 100, and export the result".
  • Paste in a long document and ask "summarize in 3 bullet points" or "extract key terms" for quick content processing.
  • You can use ChatGPT without logging in for a quick test, but creating a free account gives you conversation history and higher usage limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChatGPT completely free?

ChatGPT offers a free plan that includes access to GPT-4o with daily usage limits. For heavier use and advanced features like image generation and priority access, ChatGPT Plus is available at $20/month.

Does ChatGPT support languages other than English?

Yes, ChatGPT supports many languages including Korean, Japanese, Spanish, French, and more. You can write in your preferred language and it will respond in kind. For complex or technical tasks, English prompts tend to yield the most accurate results.

Is my conversation data used to train the model?

By default, conversations may be used to improve OpenAI's models. Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information. You can opt out under Settings → Data Controls → "Improve the model for everyone".
